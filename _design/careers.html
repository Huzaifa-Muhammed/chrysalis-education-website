Careers at Chrysalis School

Teach differently.

We are hiring qualified teachers, subject heads, and pastoral leads for our inaugural September 2026 intake. If you've ever taught online and thought "this could be so much better" — that's the conversation we want to have.

01 Why teach here

What's different about teaching at Chrysalis.

Real teaching, not crowd control

Class sizes capped at 15. You will know every student in every class by lesson three. Pastoral context comes from the Education Manager — you focus on teaching.

Built around the medium

You are not retrofitting a physical-classroom lesson for video call. You design for the medium from scratch — with technology, AV, and platform that actually work.

Subject autonomy

You shape the scheme of work for your class within the curriculum framework. We hire specialists; we trust them.

Predictable hours

No coaching sports on a Saturday morning. No supervising Year 7 on a wet break. Teaching, planning, marking — the work you signed up for.

Geographic freedom

Live wherever you want. We have teachers in the UK, UAE, Singapore, India, and rural Spain. As long as your time zone works for your timetable.

Real pay for real work

We are committed to paying senior teachers what they are worth. Salary bands are published transparently with each role.

03 How we hire

Three conversations. Then we decide together.

/01

First conversation

A 30-minute call with someone on our senior team. Mutual fit. We tell you about the school; you tell us about your work.

/02

Teaching demonstration

A 25-minute live class — your topic, your design, real students or staff. We watch you teach the way you teach.

/03

Senior team interview

Two members of the founding team, an hour. We talk about what would happen in your first term.

All written feedback is shared with you — including the reasons if we decide not to proceed. We try to make this the most respectful interview process you've ever been through, regardless of outcome.
